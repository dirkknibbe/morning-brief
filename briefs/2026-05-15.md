☀️ *Morning Brief* — Friday, May 15

The most interesting thing today isn't another harness — it's *AIMX*, a self-hosted SMTP/IMAP server designed for AI agents, with optional MCP wiring baked into the install script. The premise: stop borrowing someone else's inbox. Every long-running agent eventually needs its own addressable identity, and most orchestration stacks don't have an answer for it. This is the kind of primitive UIPE-adjacent products live next to.

🔥 *Hot Signals*
• *AIMX* (aimx.email) — `curl | sh` brings up an agent-owned mail server with STARTTLS and MCP hooks. The MCPAASTA shape is right there in the quick start.
• *ByteDance ships deer-flow* — open-source long-horizon SuperAgent harness with sandboxes, memories, tools, skills, subagents, *and a message gateway*. Worth a serious read against your current mental model for outer harnesses.
• *Emergence World* (world.emergence.ai) — 15-day multi-agent sim across 5 worlds (GPT5-mini, Claude, Gemini, Grok, mixed). Same rules, wildly divergent civilizations. Team is releasing the full dataset.
